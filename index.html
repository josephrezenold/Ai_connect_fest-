AI Prompting

Prompt It Like You See It – The Art of Reverse Creation Begins!

Overview:

         In this round, participants will be shown an AI-generated image. Their task? Use their creativity and prompting skills to recreate the image as closely as possible using an AI image generator. It's not about speed—it's about precision, observation, and how well you can reverse-engineer the prompt behind the art. Whether it's the style, subject, colors, or composition, the closer your version is to the original, the better your chances to win!

Round 1 Rules: Prompt Match Challenge

  • Objective: Replicate the given AI-generated image as closely as possible using your own prompt.
  • No Time Limit: Take your time to refine and perfect your prompt. Quality over speed.
  • Tools: Use the designated AI image generator (or your preferred one, if allowed).
  • Submission: Submit your final image and the prompt you used before the round closes.
  • Judging Criteria:
    • Visual similarity to the original
    • Prompt creativity and efficiency
    • Style, color, and subject accuracy
  • No Copying: You won’t be given the original prompt—guess, interpret, and generate on your own!
  • One Entry Only: Only your final submission will be considered.

Tips for Better Prompting

  • Be specific: Mention clear details like objects, colors, actions, or locations.
  • Describe the style: Use terms like "digital painting," "photorealistic," or "cartoon style" to guide the look.
  • Use mood or lighting: Words like “moody lighting,” “sunset glow,” or “dark shadows” help define the scene.
  • Break ideas with commas: List elements clearly to avoid jumbled results.
  • Think like a director: Imagine you're describing the scene to a visual artist or a camera crew.

Pictoword

See the Clues. Say the Word.

Overview:

         In this round, participants are challenged to decipher hidden words or phrases using visual clues. You'll be shown two or more seemingly unrelated images—but together, they form a single word when interpreted phonetically or conceptually. It’s a test of pattern recognition, vocabulary, and creative thinking. The faster and more accurately you can decode the visuals, the higher your score!

Round 2 Rules: Visual Decode Challenge

  • Objective: Decode the hidden word or phrase formed by combining the meanings or sounds of the given images.
  • Time Limit: Each puzzle has a set time limit to keep the pace energetic (e.g. 30–60 seconds per puzzle).
  • Format: Solo or team-based—depending on how competitive you want to get! (Team Limit: 2 Members per Team)
  • Answering: Submit your answers digitally or write them down if done on paper.
  • Scoring:
    • Correct answer = 1 point
    • Fastest team to submit = Bonus point
  • No Hints: You're on your own—no AI tools, just your brain and teammates.

Tips for Decoding Visuals

  • Think phonetically: Break down what the image sounds like, not just what it shows.
  • Watch word placement: Sometimes the way items are arranged (top/bottom, inside/outside) gives clues to prepositions or compound words.
  • Abstract counts too: An image of a clock might mean "time," but it could also represent "late," "tick," or "second."
  • Say it out loud: Hearing the sounds can trigger recognition faster than reading silently.
  • Stay flexible: Some puzzles are literal, some are pun-based—don’t get stuck in one mindset.

Need Help Understanding?

Your AI & Logic Game Queries Answered!

What is the goal of the AI Prompting round?

The objective is to recreate an AI-generated image as accurately as possible using your own written prompt.

Your creativity, eye for detail, and command of prompting language are key to winning.

How does the Visual Decode round work?

You’ll be shown two or more images that combine to form a word or phrase—like a visual pun or phonetic clue.

Decode the images quickly and correctly to earn points. Fastest right answers get bonus points!

Can I use AI tools during these rounds?

Only for the AI Prompting round—use the designated or allowed AI image generators to craft your prompt.

For Visual Decode, no tools allowed! Just use your brainpower and teamwork to solve the puzzles.

Can I participate in both rounds?

Absolutely! You're welcome to compete in both the AI Prompting and Visual Decode rounds.

They’re designed to test different skill sets—so go ahead and challenge yourself!
